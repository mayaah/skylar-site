Skylar vs. Caretakers

Assigning a person a caretaker who is either close personally or a paid professional often limits privacy drastically and either strains the original relationship or becomes costly very quickly. Skylar instead involves a close relation or a professional only during an emergency, significantly reducing the emotional and financial toll of the protection.

Skylar as Preemptive Care

Skylar's presence could indirectly prevent injuries. As found by the CDC, "many people who fall, even if they’re not injured, become afraid of falling. This fear may cause a person to cut down on their everyday activities. When a person is less active, they become weaker and this increases their chances of falling." Skylar’s devices also will be designed for aesthetic purposes as well as functional and be available via an a la carte system, an empowering approach to satisfying both users’ individual needs and wants.

WHY SKYLAR

We live in an unprecedented golden age for the independent spirit. Compared to 50 years ago, the average American today has 40% fewer children and is living over a decade longer, and it seems as if these trends will only continue. While the pursuit of happiness and celebration of independence are important facets of American culture, an increasing number of us are left unprepared and vulnerable in the later stages of life.

For most of the 9 million Americans living with severe disabilities and 13 million Americans aged 65 years or older living alone, life is characterized by the ability to choose.

However, due to the risk of a medical emergency, the initial reaction is to appoint a nurse, “put them in a home”, or replace individual routines with a generic formula for living longer. This reaction is understandable – a few minutes difference in response to an emergency can drastically alter its outcome. But, it highlights the presence of an ever-growing disconnect between freedom and safety.

To offer a solution that both prolongs and enriches life and to serve the millions around the world whose needs have been largely unaddressed by the modern wave of technology, Skylar is being created.
